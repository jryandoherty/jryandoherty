It's important to keep in mind the legal ramifications of choosing a payroll provider. In most cases, they'll wind up handling tax withholding from your payroll. Even though they may make the withdrawals and send the checks to the IRS, you are still responsible for making sure your payments are made correctly and on time.

Don't take chances! Whichever payroll provider you choose, check the Better Business Bureau for any potential complaints. The vast majority of payroll companies are legitimate and trustworthy, but it only takes one dishonest company to do some serious financial harm.
