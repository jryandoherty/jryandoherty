Hot tub, spa, or Jacuzzi — whatever term you use, a small pool full of steaming hot water and powerful water jets is a welcome sight at the end of a long day. Whether you're sore from a physically exhausting job, aching from sitting at a desk for hours, or just generally stressed out and looking for some relaxation, sliding in for a quick soak is just what a lot of us are looking for at the end of the day.

Having a hot tub installed is the best way to make sure you can always get that relief when you need it — but as with many major purchases, buying a hot tub can be complicated. While you may be an expert at sitting and relaxing in one, chances are you don't know everything you want to about buying a hot tub.

If you're just starting to research your hot tub purchase, there are loads of questions you'll need to answer — and they can cause enough to make you long for a relaxing soak in a hot tub, which of course you don't have yet, so you get more stressed, and … aaahh! Don't worry, we can help. Take a deep breath, relax, and let us help you answer the most important questions about your hot tub purchase:
