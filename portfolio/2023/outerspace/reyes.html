Evelyn Reyes worked at Creativity Explored from 2002 to 2017 and was known for her repeated abstract shapes in oil pastel on paper.


Reyes' work has been exhibited nationally and internationally. CB2 chose Reyes' carrots as the basis for two pillow designs, and COMME des GARÇONS selected Reyes' work for three t-shirts in 2013. In 2015, two of Reyes' artworks were selected as winning artworks in the Big-i Art Project's fifth open call.
