I'm thrilled to share with you works by Julian Kent and Emma Kohlmann.


A self-taught artist in his early twenties, Julian Kent makes imagined portraits of people caught in the complexities of everyday realities, within the scope of the Black experience. Kent sees his work as "sociological studies of human emotions."


For the past decade, Emma Kohlmann has been primarily working in watercolor and painting, creating a distinct universe in which anthropomorphous figures, flora and fauna peacefully coexist in spiritual spheres.


Outer Space would like to thank Kerry Schuss Gallery and Silke Lindner Gallery for their support in organizing this exhibition.
