About Creativity Explored

Founded in 1983 by art and disability pioneers Florence Ludins-Katz and Dr. Elias Katz, Creativity Explored's programs and person-centered culture serve as an organizational model worldwide.


Located in the Mission District of San Francisco, Creativity Explored advocates for artists' work as a valid and increasingly important contribution to the contemporary art world. As a result, studio artists have seen their work exhibited in museums, galleries, and art fairs in over 14 countries and have directly earned over $2.3 million from the sale of their art. Recent interest in artists' work illustrates the long-awaited cultural expansion of who is collected, who is discussed and who is represented.
